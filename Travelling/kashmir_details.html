Kashmir: Heaven on Earth

1. Dal Lake (Srinagar)

Dal Lake Kashmir ki jaan hai. Yahan 'Shikara' (naav) mein baithkar ghoomna aur Houseboat mein rehna ek anokha anubhav hai. Yahan ke floating markets bhi bahut mashhoor hain.

2. Gulmarg

Gulmarg ko 'Meadow of Flowers' kehte hain. Sardiyon mein yahan poori tarah barf hoti hai aur ye skiing ke liye best jagah hai. Yahan ki 'Gondola Ride' duniya ki sabse unchi cable cars mein se ek hai.

3. Pahalgam

Pahalgam apni hari-bhari ghatiyon aur Lidder nadi ke liye jana jata hai. Yahan ki Betaab Valley aur Aru Valley ki khoobsurti dekhkar aap hairan reh jayenge.

4. Sonamarg

Sonamarg ka matlab hai 'Sone ka Maidan'. Ye jagah Thajiwas Glacier ke liye mashhoor hai jahan aap saal bhar barf dekh sakte hain.

5. Shalimar Bagh

Ye ek Mughal Garden hai jise Emperor Jehangir ne apni patni Noor Jahan ke liye banwaya tha. Iski design aur fountains bahut hi shandar hain.

6. Yusmarg

Agar aapko bheed se dur shanti pasand hai, toh Yusmarg best hai. Kehte hain ki ye jagah itni sundar hai ki yahan se wapas aane ka mann nahi karta.

7. Doodhpathri

Yahan ka ghas ka maidan aur tezi se behti nadi 'Doodh' jaisi safed dikhti hai, isliye iska naam Doodhpathri pada.

8. Martand Sun Temple

Ye ek prachin mandir ke avshesh (ruins) hain. Iski purani vastukala dekhne layak hai aur ye itihas premiyon ke liye best hai.

9. Wular Lake

Ye Asia ki sabse badi fresh water lakes mein se ek hai. Yahan ka shant vatavaran aur chidiya dekhna sukoon deta hai.

10. Indira Gandhi Tulip Garden

Ye Asia ka sabse bada Tulip Garden hai. Jab yahan lakho tulips khilte hain, toh ye kisi sapne jaisa lagta hai (Ye sirf spring season mein khulta hai).
