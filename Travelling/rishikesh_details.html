Rishikesh: Yoga & Adventure Capital

1. Laxman Jhula

Laxman Jhula Rishikesh ki sabse badi pehchan hai. Ye Ganga nadi par bana ek purana jhoolta hua pul hai. Kehte hain ki Bhagwan Laxman ne yahi se rassi ke sahare nadi par ki thi. Yahan se nadi ka najara bahut sundar lagta hai.

2. Triveni Ghat

Ye Rishikesh ka sabse bada ghat hai. Shaam ke waqt yahan hone wali Ganga Aarti dekhne layak hoti hai. Yahan log pavitra snaan ke liye aate hain.

3. Ram Jhula

Ye Laxman Jhula se thoda bada hai aur Swarg Ashram aur Shivananda Ashram ko jodta hai. Yahan hamesha bhakt aur paryatakon ki bheed rehti hai.
